Minature Schnauzers


Cute Schanuzer

About

Miniature Schnauzers are loyal, smart, and hyper dogs. They love food and attention.

They work well with other animals and children

Be wary they do love to Bark!


Caring For Your Dog

Schanuzers need some extra attention for their fur and diet.

Make sure to keep them udpated on their shots!

Some food that is good as treats (all food should be given in moderation):

  • Carrots
  • Peanuts
  • Apples
  • Bananas

Feeding Schedule Weight Quantity
0-5lbs 1/3 cup
6-10lbs 1/2 cup
10lb or More 3/4 cup
